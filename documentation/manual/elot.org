#+BEGIN_QUOTE
ELOT lets you author OWL ontologies in Org-mode, exporting to OWL
Manchester Syntax, HTML and PDF documentation, and running SPARQL,
ROBOT and rdfpuml against them from inside Emacs.
#+END_QUOTE
